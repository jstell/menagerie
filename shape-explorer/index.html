📚 Formula Reference (Virginia SOL)

▼

🟥 Rectangle

  • Area = l × w
  • Perimeter = 2l + 2w (or 2(l+w))

🔺 Triangle

  • Area = ½ × b × h
  • Perimeter = a + b + c

⭕ Circle

  • Area = π × r²
  • Circumference = 2π × r

🔵 Semicircle

  • Area = (π × r²) ÷ 2
  • Arc = π × r

💡 Composite Shapes

  • Add method: A + B + C
  • Sub method: Whole − Cutout
  • Perimeter: Sum of outer sides only
