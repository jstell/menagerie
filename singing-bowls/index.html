Playing the bowls

  • Sing — press a bowl and drag round and round the rim, like a wet finger on a wineglass. Circle faster to swell louder; stop circling to let it ring out.
  • Strike — a quick click taps the bowl for a bell-like tone.
  • Flick — a fast little swipe across the rim gives a brighter, shorter attack.
  • Material — switch metal / crystal / glass / ceramic up top; each rings differently.
  • Bigger bowls on the left sound lower. Play several at once.
  • Wooden friend — each scene keeps one wooden instrument at the lower right (temple fish, bamboo chimes, tongue drum, log drum). Tap it; drag across the chimes to strum.
  • Auto-play — the play button up top lets the bowls play themselves, slow and meditative.
  • Ambience — the wave button toggles each scene's background soundscape; scenes also change the reverb around the bowls.

Each bowl is synthesized live from its inharmonic vibration modes, with two slightly-detuned voices per mode for the gentle beating shimmer real bowls have.
